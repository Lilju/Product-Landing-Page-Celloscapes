Celloscapes, a relaxing cello music trilogy by Lilju

Features

Cello music

The concept for these albums was to create music tracks which would describe different landscapes, imaginary or real, mostly inspired by the landscapes I visited in Ireland and Iceland. I composed music tracks inspired by celtic and medieval music in modal tones.

Binaural Beats

I studied the relaxing properties of binaural beats to mix them with my tracks, to get a deeper soothing effect when listened with headphones.

White Noise

I also recorded nature sounds when travelling, mostly white noise (wind and water) which also has a soothing effect for even deeper relaxation.
